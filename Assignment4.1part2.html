The history of My college class storytelling

It was in my First class in the second semester, we were doing an assignment on testing inputs and outputs in python coding, any I thought, why not make an interactive story out of this. And so, I got to work on my very first app dubbed "The Saturn Expirement". this story included a post apocolyptic world infested with mutants, however with peaceful ones, and we also follow a character where he/she is transported to the same future. The success afterwards felt amazing so I then procceed to do the same to every code I do and this transitioned to an adventure story, another post apocolyptic story, and even an action story. today I thought I would share a bit of that history with you in this site called "Broken Tavern Tales" In which is dedicated to one-shot stories in this page.
